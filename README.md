## Objective
To demonstrate hands-on understanding of ISO/IEC 27001 risk assessment and treatment
using an Excel-based risk register.

## Scope

- No real production or client data used

## Methodology
- Defined impact and likelihood criteria
- Identified assets, threats, and vulnerabilities
- Calculated inherent risk scores
- Selected risk treatment options
- Identified residual risk after controls

## Standards Referenced
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- ISO/IEC 27005 (Risk Management)
