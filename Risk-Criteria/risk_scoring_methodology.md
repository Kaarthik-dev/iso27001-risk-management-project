# Risk Scoring Methodology

## Impact Scale
| Score | Description |
|------:|-------------|
| 1 | Negligible |
| 2 | Minor |
| 3 | Moderate |
| 4 | Major |
| 5 | Critical |

## Likelihood Scale
| Score | Description |
|------:|-------------|
| 1 | Rare |
| 2 | Unlikely |
| 3 | Possible |
| 4 | Likely |
| 5 | Almost Certain |

## Risk Score Calculation
Risk Score = Impact × Likelihood

## Risk Levels
| Score Range | Risk Level |
|------------:|-----------|
| 1–5 | Low |
| 6–12 | Medium |
| 15–25 | High |

## Review
Risk assessments are reviewed periodically or when significant changes occur.
